Our Core Services

Comprehensive energy solutions designed to power Ghana's development and ensure sustainable growth for all sectors of the economy.

Electricity Generation

Operating major hydroelectric facilities including Akosombo and Bui dams, providing reliable baseload power generation for Ghana's national grid with over 1,200MW capacity.

Renewable Energy Development

Pioneering clean energy initiatives through solar, wind, and small hydro projects to diversify Ghana's energy mix and reduce carbon footprint for sustainable development.

Industrial Power Solutions

Providing specialized power solutions for industrial customers, mining operations, and large commercial enterprises with reliable, cost-effective electricity supply agreements.

Water Management

Managing Ghana's water resources through our reservoir systems, supporting irrigation, fisheries, and water supply while maintaining optimal power generation levels.

Training & Development

Building capacity in the energy sector through technical training programs, research initiatives, and knowledge transfer to develop local expertise and innovation.

Community Development

Implementing social responsibility programs that support education, healthcare, and economic development in communities affected by our operations and infrastructure projects.
